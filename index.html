About Arcane

Arcane tells the story of characters living in two very different cities: the wealthy, advanced Piltover and the struggling, dangerous undercity of Zaun. The show explores themes like power, morality, and the choices that change lives as tensions rise between these cities. Piltover's invention of "hextech," a tool that lets anyone use magic, and Zaun's creation of "shimmer," a drug that turns people into monsters, fuel the conflict. Families and friendships are torn apart, as we see the lives of beloved League of Legends characters like Vi, Jinx, Caitlyn, Jayce, Viktor, and Ekko unfold.
