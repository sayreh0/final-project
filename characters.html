Meet the Cast

Vi

The fiery enforcer from Piltover, Vi grew up in Zaun's undercity. She is determined to protect her city and its people.

Jinx

Vi's unpredictable sister, Jinx, is a brilliant inventor turned anarchist who thrives on chaos in Zaun.

Jayce

A visionary inventor, Jayce is a symbol of progress and innovation in Piltover, using Hextech to change the world.

Caitlyn

Caitlyn Kiramman is a skilled enforcer from Piltover and the daughter of one of Piltover's wealthiest families. She is dedicated to justice and is known for her strong moral compass.

Ekko

Ekko is a brilliant and resourceful inventor from Zaun. His creativity and determination make him a key figure in the fight for a better future.

Ambessa

Ambessa Medarda is the mother of Mel Medarda, Piltover's influential councilor. She is a high ranking warlord from Noxus. Ambessa's presence in Arcane introduces the political and military might of Noxus.
